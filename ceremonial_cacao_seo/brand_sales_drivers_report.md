## Suggestions for Agroverse.shop

Based on what is driving sales for the top 5 competitors, here are concrete actions Agroverse can take, in priority order:

### High priority (all 5 competitors do this)

1. **Own site as flagship** — Every brand leads with their site. Ensure agroverse.shop has a clear **Shop / Products** path, fast load, and mobile-friendly checkout.
2. **Wholesale / B2B** — **4 of 5** brands have a dedicated wholesale (or trade) page. Add a clear **Wholesale** or **For Retailers** page with contact or application flow so retailers and venues can find you.
3. **Messaging** — Use **organic, ceremonial, single-origin, regenerative**, and **direct/farm traceability** consistently on the site and in any retailer listings (you already differentiate on Amazon regeneration and farm traceability; double down).

### Medium priority (most competitors do this)

4. **Subscription** — **3 of 5** offer subscription / subscribe & save. If inventory allows, offer a **subscription option** on key SKUs to capture recurring revenue and match customer expectations.
5. **Free shipping threshold** — **2 of 5** promote a clear free-shipping threshold (e.g. over $95–$100). Add a **prominent free-shipping threshold** on homepage and cart (e.g. “Free US shipping on orders over $X”) to reduce cart abandonment.
6. **Where to buy / Stockists** — **Ma Cacao** stands out with a dedicated **Retailers** page. Add a **“Find us” or “Stockists”** page and keep it updated; it builds trust and supports wholesale and in-store discovery.

### Growth and reach

7. **Multi-channel presence** — Ora Cacao (Ceremonial Cacao) is also sold via **Bar and Cocoa** and likely other retailers; their brand appears in multiple places. Consider getting Agroverse listed on a **curated retailer** (e.g. Bar and Cocoa) or evaluating **Amazon** for discovery, while keeping agroverse.shop as the primary brand home.
8. **Site reliability** — Ora’s primary site (oracacao.com) had load timeouts in past research. Keep agroverse.shop **fast and stable** so the brand is always reachable when people search.
